Dining Out

Other than enjoying the great outdoors and relaxing, Big Pine also has some of the best food in California. From Country Kitchen Diner to the famous Erick Schat's Bakery in Bishop California, you cannot miss out on the food you can get here.

Country Kitchen Diner

From the traditional Breakfasts, to the authentic Mexican food, Country Kitchen Diner is a must if you are every on vacation in Big Pine. The owners are locals of Big Pine and are some of the kindest, warm-hearted individuals you could ever wish to meet. Their staff is very professional and the ingredients are all fresh.

Copper Top BBQ

Copper Top BBQ is a world class BBQ restaurant. It is impossible to be in Big Pine and not smell the amazing BBQ. Tri-Tip, Pulled Pork, Buttered Chicken Bites and Ribs, Copper Top BBQ has all of your BBQ craves handled. Fresh ingredients, friendly staff as well as a great seating layout, this place is a must to visit.

Erick Schat's Bakery

The Erick Schat's Story from: https://www.schats.com/our-story

Erick Schat's Bakery is another must visit location. This particular location has been open in Owens Valley since 1938. The Bakery has bread with all sorts of toppings, pastries, donuts, gelato's, and a sandwich shop all located under 1 roof. Be warned though, this place gets packed and 20 to 30 minute wait-times during Covid19 are common but totally worth it.
